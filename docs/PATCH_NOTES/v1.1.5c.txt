## Highlights
- Migrated Streamlit layout calls to the new `width` argument to silence
  `use_container_width` deprecation warnings across overlay, archive, and
  similarity views.
- Enumerated duplicate trace labels in similarity matrices with alias captions
  so pyarrow export/rendering succeeds even when identical labels are present.
- Rebuilt overlay plotting to regenerate missing `wavelength_m` columns from
  nanometre grids, preventing crashes when legacy payloads skip metre units.
